# Statistics Note: Onboarding Experiment

## Experiment

Two onboarding flows were compared during a one-week internal experiment.

- Group A used the current onboarding flow.
- Group B used the new guided onboarding flow.

## Results

| Group | Visitors | Activated users | Activation rate |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| A | 1000 | 400 | 40% |
| B | 1000 | 460 | 46% |

The absolute lift for Group B is 6 percentage points. The relative lift is 15%
because `6 / 40 = 0.15`.

## Interpretation guidance

Do not call the result final without more context. The note does not include a
p-value, confidence interval, acquisition channel mix, or retention outcome.
The safest conclusion is that Group B performed better in this sample, but the
experiment needs statistical validation before rollout.
