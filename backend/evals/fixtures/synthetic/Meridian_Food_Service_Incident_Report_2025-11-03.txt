Meridian Food Service AB
Incident Report
Document date: 2025-11-03

Incident summary
On 2025-11-02, the overnight refrigeration monitor triggered alert code Frost Ember at the Lund distribution kitchen.

Findings
The compressor room temperature rose above the approved threshold for 47 minutes.
No injuries were reported, but two product batches were quarantined pending inspection.

Recommendation
Replace the temperature relay within seven days and review the overnight escalation checklist.
