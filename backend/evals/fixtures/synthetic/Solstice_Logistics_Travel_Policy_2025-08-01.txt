Travel Reimbursement Policy
Policy date: 2025-08-01

Purpose
This policy explains how employees are reimbursed for approved business travel.

Hotel Reimbursement
Employees may book hotels up to EUR 180 per night in standard locations.
Managers may approve higher rates for peak season or remote delivery sites.

Mileage
Mileage is reimbursed at EUR 0.42 per kilometer for approved use of private vehicles.

Submission
Claims must be submitted within 14 days after the trip together with receipts.
