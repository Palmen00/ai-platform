Northstar Aerotech Ltd
Master Service Agreement
Effective date: 2026-01-15

Parties
Northstar Aerotech Ltd and Fjordbyte Analytics AB enter into this master service agreement for project support, reporting, and software maintenance.

Project
This agreement covers Project Nebula Arc, which includes telemetry dashboards, flight-data parsing, and incident review workflows.

Renewal
The agreement renews automatically every 12 months unless either party gives 60 days written notice before the renewal date.

Payment Terms
Invoices are due within 30 days. The monthly retainer is EUR 8,500 excluding VAT.

Confidentiality
Both parties must keep customer data, maintenance notes, and telemetry exports confidential.
