# Settings Guide

Settings in Local AI OS is an admin-oriented control panel.

Typical areas include:

- Overview: app health, dependency status, storage footprint, and readiness.
- Runtime: model and retrieval-related configuration.
- Connectors: external content sources and sync controls.
- Security: auth posture, safe mode, and security-related controls.
- Users: local accounts and role management.
- Audit: sensitive activity history.
- Storage and Cleanup: disk usage and cleanup actions.
- Backups and Logs: export/import and operational inspection.

Important behavior:

- Some settings require admin auth.
- Safe mode can block higher-risk actions such as cleanup or connector changes.
- User and document access rules affect what different accounts can see.
